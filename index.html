Emily & Anders

Emily Seitz and Anders Lee met at MIT in the fall of 2010, becoming friends during the spring and summer of 2011. After starting their relationship in October 2011, they've endured 2.5 years of long-distance dating between Philly, Boston, and Austin. On August 30, 2014, at Walden Pond outside of Boston, Anders proposed and Emily said yes! They are very excited for the beginning of their marriage this summer and to move together to San Jose, CA, after the wedding. Anders will begin his career as an electrical engineer, and Emily will continue to design educational software.
